# FITNESS SM Mini Brand System

## Brand Direction

FITNESS SM is positioned as a premium feminine performance app: calm, disciplined, elegant, and personal. The identity avoids literal gym equipment and uses a soft monogram language to express motion, rhythm, strength, and progress.

## Logo Concept

Primary mark:
- Abstract `SM` monogram.
- Frosted lavender and pearl gradient.
- Rounded-square iOS app icon composition.
- Luxury-tech finish with glass highlights.

Brand feeling:
- Apple Fitness+ meets feminine wellness brand.
- Minimal, expensive, and soft.
- Strong enough at iPhone home-screen size.

## Palette

- Pearl White: `#FFFFFF`
- Lavender Mist: `#F4F0FF`
- Soft Lilac: `#C9B6FF`
- Royal Lavender: `#8F6FE8`
- Frosted Purple: `#A47DF0`
- Deep Plum: `#22172E`
- Silver Glow: `rgba(255,255,255,0.72)`
- Blush Accent: `#F5C8D7`

## App Integration

Implemented across:
- PWA icon and Apple touch icon.
- App manifest.
- Splash screen.
- Dashboard top brand mark.
- Today's workout chip.
- Workout screen mini wordmark.
- Progress header.
- Measurements header.
- Settings header.
- Navigation center action style.

## UI Accent Style

Use the logo sparingly:
- Top-level identity moments.
- Empty states.
- Completion and achievement states.
- PWA install icon.

Avoid repeating the mark inside every card. Luxury branding works best when it has breathing room.
